Who We Are

We don't just photograph weddings — we preserve legacies.

The KalyaanamFrames is a boutique photography and cinematography studio born from a deep love for South Indian culture and the timeless beauty of its celebrations.

Founded in 2024 and based in Karimnagar, we have had the honor of being part of over 100 weddings, engagements, and family stories across Telangana, Andhra Pradesh, and beyond.

Our aesthetic blends editorial precision with warm, emotional storytelling — creating images and films that feel as real and beautiful as the moments themselves.
